Who can use meal mill

Individuals

Individuals looking to lose or gain weight can use Meal Mill for fully customizable meal plans. Long gone are the days of boring meal plans. With Meal Mill, you can add flavor to your diet and become your own nutritionist.

Coaches

Designing customized meal plans for clients can be a time consuming process. Coaches can use Meal Mill to greatly improve the meal planning process. Coaches also have the luxury of over 5000 meals to choose from, all with comprehensive caloric profiles.

How it works

Its simple

  • You tell us some basic information

    We take into account your age, weight, height, gender, activity level and goals

  • We calculate your caloric requirements

    Based on the information provided, we create a caloric profile and estimate your caloric needs

  • Based on your needs, we mill out a meal plan

    The reccomended meal plans are in line with you goal and are rich in vitamins and minerals

  • Not happy with the meal plan? Regenerate as many times as you like!

    That's right, unlimited meal plans appearing instantly
